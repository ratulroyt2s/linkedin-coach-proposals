Inquiries Handled Manually via Phone & Email

Energy Tenants Comparing Multiple Properties

Energy companies evaluating Permian Basin industrial space aren't waiting around. If they're comparing three properties and IWI Realty doesn't have a systematic follow-up sequence, your property gets forgotten in favor of whoever stayed top of mind.
